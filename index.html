For Services

I am Marjorie Salazar Pariñas, a mural artist passionate about transforming empty walls into vibrant masterpieces. With a love for wildlife, nature, and cultural themes, I create art that tells a story, turning spaces into captivating environments. Working with mixed media, I bring flexibility and creativity to every project, ensuring each mural is unique and tailored to reflect the vision of the space. Let’s bring your walls to life with art that inspires and transforms!

Feel free to reach out for a quote. Let’s elevate your walls into stunning masterpiece.

About Me

Welcome to About Page

Name: Marjorie Salazar Parinas
Age: 26
Birth date: March 14, 1998
Address: San Carlos Binangonan Rizal

I began my mural artist journey on January 1, 2021. My artistic path has been shaped by my innate talent and refined through hands-on experiences. Inspired by the vibrant competition within the art scene, I strive to become one of the well-known artists in the world.

My love for adventures and wildlife greatly influences my art, allowing me to bring the beauty of nature and the thrill of exploration to life on walls. I believe that art has the power to transform spaces and evoke emotions, and I am committed to growing and exceeding my potential as an artist.

My Mission and Vision

Mission

I aim to inspire future generations by creating art that highlights the beauty of the natural world and raises awareness about wildlife conservation. My goal is to evoke wonder in God's creation, transforming walls into vibrant masterpieces.

Vision

To be a globally recognized mural artist known for my commitment to cleanliness, accuracy, speed, and quality of an artwork.
